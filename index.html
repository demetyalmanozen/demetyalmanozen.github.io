Who Am I?

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering and a member of Center for Wireless Information Processing (CWiP) at NJIT.

Prior to joining NJIT, I worked at Vodafone Turkey as a Network Engineer. I earned an M.Sc. degree from the Department of Mathematical Sciences at Georgia Southern University, GA and a B.Sc. degree from the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at Cankaya University, Turkey. I also hold another B.Sc. degree from the Department of Electronic and Communication Engineering at Cankaya University.

I am working under the guidance of Professor Ali Abdi. My research mainly focuses on Computational and Systems Biology. I am particularly interested in developing methods to analyze large signaling networks using experimental data. My research interests also include molecular networks, cell signaling, optimization, mathematical modeling and graph theory.
